Software Engineer Student

I’m Raffaele Colantuono, a 26 years old Computer Science Engineer Student at Federico II University. I have a quiet personality, getting along with everybody and managing to listen carefully to what people say and understanding their point of view thanks to my empathy. I’m usually very critical to myself, and this aspect keeps me to grow and to better myself. Passionate about Technology and Videogames since childhood, I made all choices to reach my dream of becoming a Software Developer and one day make Videogames. In the last year, I have attended the "Java per la Campania" formation course,  getting  Java SE 8 Certification by Oracle and increasing my knowledge about SQL, UML, and OOP.  Immediately after I attend of course the Apple Developer Academy,  during which I improved my design, coding, and soft skills, concluding the journey with 3 App on AppStore, one of them as a personal Game Project. I spend my free time taking photos of Nature and Moon with my reflex, playing acoustic and electric guitar and sometimes cooking.
