Hello!

I’m Raffaele Colantuono, a 26 years old Computer Science Engineer Student at Federico II University. Passionate about Technology and Videogames since childhood, I made all choices to reach my dream of becoming a Software Developer and one day make Videogames. I have attended an Oracle Java formation course,  getting  Java SE 8 Certification and increasing my knowledge about SQL, UML, and OOP.  Immediately after I attend of course the Apple Developer Academy,  during which I improved my design, coding, and soft skills, concluding the journey with 3 App on AppStore, one of them as a personal Game Project. I spend my free time taking photos of Nature and Moon with my reflex, playing acoustic and electric guitar and sometimes cooking.
