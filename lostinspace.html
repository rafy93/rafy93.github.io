Lost in Space: Dimensions

Explore space and all his dimensions with Lost in Space!

Pilot your spaceship in deep space and avoid planets, asteroids and black holes by collecting power ups and coins. Search the portals, they will take you to another dimension!

5 Different Dimensions

With Lost in Space you can choose different dimensions like "Modern" with a flat aspect, the classical "Retro", a colored "Pixel", a drawn by hand "Childhood" and a bright "Neon" style. There is something for everyone, just choose one and start a new journey!

Collect Power Ups

Increase your score using power ups such as magnets, speed-up, shield and double multiplier but be careful, they are temporary!

Chose your way to Play

Control your spaceship with a simple drag on the screen or rotating the device thanks to gyroscope.

Unlock all Trophies

Challenge yourself with 25 different trophies and gain all 1000 points on Game Center

Be #1 in HighScore Leaderboard

Two different controls, two different Leaderboard! Compete with all the World and score the highest to be number one.
